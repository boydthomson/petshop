# AI-Generated Videos for Pet Shop Game

This folder should contain AI-generated videos of people playing with different animals.

## Required Videos

Place these 4 video files in this folder:

1. **dog-playing.mp4**
   - AI-generated video of a person playing fetch with a dog
   - Should be about 30 seconds long
   - Show person throwing a ball/toy and dog fetching it

2. **cat-playing.mp4**
   - AI-generated video of a person playing with a cat
   - Should be about 30 seconds long
   - Show person using a toy (like a string or feather toy) with a cat

3. **rabbit-playing.mp4**
   - AI-generated video of a person playing with a rabbit
   - Should be about 30 seconds long
   - Show person petting and interacting with a rabbit

4. **hamster-playing.mp4**
   - AI-generated video of a person playing with a hamster
   - Should be about 30 seconds long
   - Show person holding and playing with a hamster

## How to Generate These Videos

You can use AI video generation tools like:
- **Runway ML** (runwayml.com)
- **Pika Labs** (pika.art)
- **Stable Video Diffusion**
- **OpenAI Sora** (when available)

### Example Prompts:

**For dog video:**
"A person playing fetch with a golden retriever dog in a park, throwing a tennis ball, the dog running and bringing it back, happy and playful, cinematic, 30 seconds"

**For cat video:**
"A person playing with an orange tabby cat using a feather toy, the cat jumping and pawing at the toy, indoors, playful and cute, 30 seconds"

**For rabbit video:**
"A person gently petting and playing with a white fluffy rabbit on the floor, the rabbit hopping around, calm and gentle interaction, 30 seconds"

**For hamster video:**
"A person holding and playing with a small hamster in their hands, the hamster exploring and being petted, cute and gentle, close-up view, 30 seconds"

## Video Format

- **Format:** MP4
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 (1080p) or 1280x720 (720p)
- **Duration:** ~30 seconds each
- **Codec:** H.264
